FORMATION DRAFTS

This is an Automated live Script that chooses the best available(Injury free) players to start in your FPL team based on ICT (Influence, Creativity, Threat) and Form for all FPL Formations. It follows all the FPL rules (3 players max from a particular team, budget of £100M, etc). This only picks the starting 11, Use the remaining budget to pick your bench.

FORMATION SUMMARY

Formation name
The name of the Formationhelp
Team value
The monetary value of the Team from the live transfer market in £help
Ict form
Statistical index developed specifically to assess a player as an FPL asset, combining Influence, Creativity and Threat scores and Form is a player’s average score per match, calculated from all matches played by his club in the last 30 days.help
Remaining Budget
The money to spend on your bench playershelp
